Providing affordable and accessible healthcare products to the community.

Site Name

Caszmeds – The brand name for the pharmacy, focusing on healthcare product accessibility.

Site Purpose

The Caszmeds website provides information about our pharmacy, highlights featured products, shares daily health tips, and allows visitors to contact us for assistance.

Visitor Scenarios

  • Which medicines are available for cold and flu?
  • How can I contact Caszmeds for advice or prescription inquiries?

Color Scheme

Primary: #005B96 – Headings, links, buttons

Secondary: #F4F4F4 – Background sections

Accent: #007ACC – Subtle highlights, button hover

Typography

  • Bebas Neue: headings, page titles
  • Open Sans: body text, paragraphs
  • Roboto: optional labels or small notes

Wireframes

Mobile and desktop wireframes will illustrate the homepage layout including header, featured products, health tips, and contact section.
